Laura Nedved

My degree is in Communication, my life is in technology. I love finding creative solutions to bridge that challenging gap; distilling ambiguous, messy problems down into user-friendly, easy-to-understand solutions.

I've been fortunate! I've been exposed to technology, tools and concepts across many organizations which means I've been able to partner with some amazing and talented people. And, I brew a pretty decent kombucha!

I am constantly intrigued by questions like,

“Why do we do the things we do?”

“Is there a better way to do this?”

This is especially true for how we engage with technology.
